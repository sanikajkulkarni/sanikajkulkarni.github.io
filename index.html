Hobbies

Singing

I started singing at age four, and it has been a constant in my life. I realized in high school that it has become one of my passion. I love singing as a means to express myself, destress, and challenge myself as there is always something new to learn and improve on.

Writing

Writing has also become one of my favorite hobbies. After trying my hand at creative writing in school, my teachers encouraged me to continue writing even outside school. I've realized I enjoy it for the same reasons I enjoy singing; alongside those reasons, I also love how many genres and styles of writing I can explore.

Crochet

I began crocheting around age nine. One of my friend's moms taught me my first basic stitches. Since then, it has become one of my favorite hobbies to destress. I also love seeing how many different things I can create with it - from clothes, to stuffed animals and accessories, the posibilities are endless!

Reading

Reading, alongside singing, is one of the hobbies I cultivated earliest. I love being able to immerse myself in different perspectives and experience worlds I may never see in real life. Becuase of this, my favorite genres are sci-fi, mythology, and romance.
